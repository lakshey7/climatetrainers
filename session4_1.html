Batteries
Learning Objectives
  • Explain how a battery works
  • Explain how batteries are rated
  • Predict the voltage, current and energy from a battery bank in different configurations
  • Describe the charging and discharging characteristics of batteries
  • Explain the differences and trade-offs between different types of batteries
  • Choose the right type of battery for a given application
Duration: 10 hours including repetition and reinforcement
