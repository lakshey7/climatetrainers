Wiring in PV Systems
Learning Objectives
  • Recognize the different types and sizes of wires and cables used in solar PV systems
  • Recognize the different types and sizes of protection devices used in a solar PV system
  • Use a decision table to pick the right sizes for wires and protection devices for a PV system configuration
Duration: 6 hours including repetition and reinforcement

Introduction

The wires used in PV system will play a significant part in the overall efficiency. An installer not only be able to recognize the different types of wires and cables, but also have an understanding of why wire thickness is an important factor

 

Play Slides

  • Solar PV System Wiring

 

Conduct Activities

  1. Provide 3 different SLD of PV systems to the learners. From all the cables, wires and breakers available in the lab, they should pick the right ones mentioned in the SLD
  2. Provide PV configurations of different system sizes. Use the wire sizing chart provided in the learner handout, to pick the right wire size for all the parts of the system
Ask and Discuss
  • What are the consequences of undersized wires?
  • What are the consequences of oversized wires?
  • Is the same wire thickness used for AC and DC wiring if the current is the same?
