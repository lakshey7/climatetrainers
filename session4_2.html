Introduction

PV modules will produce power whenever there is sunlight. But what if the batteries are full? You will then need a charge controller. How do they work? What are the different mechanisms available?

 

Play Slides

  • Charge Controllers

 

Activities

  1. Open a PWM and MPPT charge controller and create a simple schematic diagram of the components
  2. Connect the charge controller and note the input and output current values periodically along with the battery voltage. Observer how the current changes with the battery state of charge. You can repeat this for PWM and MPPT charge controllers

Introduction

Inverters are the heart of a solar system. This is the component that interacts with the load. How do they work? What are the different mechanisms available?

 

Play Slides

  • Inverters

 

Activities

  1. View an inverter data sheet and note all parameters in a notebook
  2. Open a solar inverter and create a simple schematic diagram of the internal components
  3. Read the output of an inverter from a data logger and discuss the meaning of the different data points
Ask and Discuss
  • What are the main electronic components inside a charge controller?
  • What is the technical specification of a charge controller?
  • What are the different types of charge controller and their pros and cons?
  • How is a grid-tied inverter different from an off-grid inverter?
  • What are the technical parameters in an inverter specification?
