Introduction

Why is it necessary to survey the site before designing the PV system? What data will you need to gather? Site assessment is the process of gathering data from the site before doing a design. It is done early in the sales process.

Conduct On-site Activity

  • Learners should form groups of 2 and draw a layout of available space for different components. Mark areas on the site suitable for placement of the solar array, inverter, battery and wiring routes. Discuss the layout diagrams

Introduction

It is easy to understand that shading prevents direct radiation from falling on the PV module and reduces output. So it is very important to ensure that shadows do not fall on the panel in the summer or the winter. But how will you know if there will be shadows on a particular spot in the winter or the summer?
