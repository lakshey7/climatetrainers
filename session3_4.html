Introduction

When you want to install a solar system for the consumer, will you consider energy or power requirement? Does the customer want to meet all their requirements with solar energy? In the next few slides, you will learn the terminology and steps needed to answer this question

 

Play Slides

Conclude

You need to consider both energy and load when designing a solar system. What you use will depend on the type of system. However, at the time of site survey it is important to capture as much information as possible.

Introduction

You have seen how to assess the load and energy use for a customer. Now, you can do this yourself for this building.

 

Conduct Activity

Divide into groups of 2 to independently conduct the load assessment for the building. Use the load assessment standard task procedure (STP)

  • STP for Load Assessment

 

Conclude

Each group will reveal the following results from their activity

  • Peak load
  • Average load
  • Critical load
  • Daily energy requirement
