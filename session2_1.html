Site Activity

Measure solar radiation at different times of the day using a pyranometer. Create a logbook and discuss the differences based on time of day and weather conditions.

 

Conclude

When thinking of solar radiation, you should ask yourself the following questions: What type? In which latitude? How much?

Ask and Discuss
  • Does more heat energy provide more electrical output from solar PV or less?
  • Will a solar PV panel provide any output from diffuse radiation?
  • To provide 1 kilowatt of output, will the size of your solar array depend on the latitude?
  • What factors affect radiation other than latitude?
