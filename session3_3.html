Introduction

What customer needs are to be considered when designing a solar system? Let us learn through some scenarios. After studying the scenarios, you can create a decision table, that details what customer needs are met through what type of system

Suggested Activities

  • Learners create customer needs profile for their own institute by interviewing the facility manager and listing their need
  • Talk to the households and shops in your neighborhood about installing a solar system. Discuss the results in your class

 

Conclude

  • Fitting the right system type to the right customer need is very important. The right system type is influenced by factors such as:
  • Backup requirement
  • Grid availability
  • Budget
  • Space

Introduction

You have studied about the different PV system types and customer needs. Sometimes after you assess customer needs, you will need to create simple block diagrams to explain the solution to your customers or peers. In such a block diagram, you can explain scenarios important to the customer. Let us see how to do that.

Ask and Discuss
  • What customer needs is satisfied through an AC off-grid system?
  • What customer needs is satisfied through a DC off-grid system?
  • What customer needs is satisfied through a grid-tied system?
  • For rural areas with a lot of power cuts, will a grid tied system be a good option?
  • In a grid-tied system, does solar directly charge batteries?
  • Can a stand-alone system directly supply to the load, if the battery is discharged?
