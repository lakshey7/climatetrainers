Solar PV System Types
Learning Objectives

  • Predict how each system will behave under scenarios such as overloading, battery charging or power cuts

Ask and Discuss
  • What happens when the batteries are fully charged in a stand-alone system and there is still plenty of solar power available?
  • What happens in a stand-alone system when the battery is charging and the load is on?
  • In a grid-tied system, what happens when there is a power cut?
  • In a grid-tied system, what happens when solar power is not enough to satisfy the load requirement?
