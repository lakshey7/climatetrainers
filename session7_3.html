System Diagnosis and Troubleshooting
Learning Objectives
  • Follow a systematic procedure to troubleshoot the system by reading a troubleshooting table
  • Work through basic troubleshooting steps for inverters, batteries, PV modules and wiring
  • Describe problems that typically occur in all components and their causes
  • Fill a job card to ensure the problem and solution are documented
Duration: 40 hours including on-site activities

Introduction

When there is a problem with a system what steps will you first take? When a doctor meets a patient with a problem what steps does he or she take? It is important to follow a systematic procedure to gather data and estimate the cause of the problem
