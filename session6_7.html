Introduction

The short circuit current and open circuit voltage of a string indicates the health of the modules in the string. It saves you time as you cannot always check the health of each module individually. Perform on-site activity to test Voc and Isc of individual modules and array. Compare and discuss results from different learners.

Ask and Discuss

What are the consequences of the following problems if they occur in a site inspection?

  • Panels not accessible for cleaning
  • Improper earthing
  • Component ratings do not match design
  • Wiring conduits are not supported properly
  • There is no ventilation in the battery room
