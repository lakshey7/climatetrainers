Electrical Basics
Learning Objectives
  • Explain the concept of voltage, current, power and energy
  • Create series and parallel configuration with loads and predict the voltage and current
  • Explain the function of electrical components like fuses, capacitors and circuit breakers
  • Create simple electrical circuits by reading circuit diagrams

Begin

How does the current vary when the resistance is changed across a circuit with constant voltage? What do you think will happen when the load connected to a solar PV module is changed? To answer this, we need to start with a clear understanding of voltage, current and power.

Conclude

PV cells deliver electrical energy in the form of voltage and current. A good understanding of voltage and current is therefore important.
